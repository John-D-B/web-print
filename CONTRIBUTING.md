# Contributing to Web-Print

Thanks for your interest in improving **Web-Print**.<br/>
Contributions are welcome: bug reports, fixes, and features.

## Inbound licensing (please read before submitting)

**Web-Print** is offered under two licenses: the Business Source License 1.1<br/>
&nbsp; &nbsp; (BSL), and a commercial license from Mountain Informatik GmbH (see `LICENSING.md`).

That dual model works only while a single entity holds the rights to license<br/>
&nbsp; &nbsp; the whole work under both. By submitting a pull request or patch, you agree that:

- **Right to submit:**<br/>
you are the author of the contribution, or otherwise have the right to submit it.

- **Dual grant:**<br/>
you license your contribution to Mountain Informatik GmbH under the BSL,<br/>
&nbsp; &nbsp; and grant permission to include it in commercially licensed versions of **Web-Print**.

- **You keep your copyright:**<br/>
this is a license grant, not an assignment; you retain copyright in your contribution.

In short: **inbound = the same dual terms as outbound.**<br/>
This keeps **Web-Print** distributable under both licenses<br/>
&nbsp; &nbsp; without having to track down past contributors later.

If you cannot agree to these terms, please open an issue to discuss<br/>
&nbsp; &nbsp; before submitting code, rather than sending a pull request.

## Practical notes

- Keep changes focused: one topic per pull request.
- Match the existing code style.
- For security-relevant changes, see `SECURITY.md`.
